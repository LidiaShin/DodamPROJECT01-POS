K-Pop Survey Form

K-POP is the short form of Korean popular music. Originated in the early 1990s, K-POP is now a generic term refers to all genres of "popular music" within South Korea such as rapping, dance-pop, pop ballad, electronic, rock, hip-hop, R&B etc. For instance, “Gangnam Style” from PSY and “Nobody” from Wonder Girls are two of the most well known K-pop songs around the world.
